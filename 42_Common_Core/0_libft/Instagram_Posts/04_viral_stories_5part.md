## Technical Specifications

### File Formats:
- **Video**: MP4, 1080x1920px (9:16 ratio)
- **Images**: PNG/JPG, same dimensions
- **Duration**: 15 seconds per story maximum
- **Audio**: High-quality, royalty-free coding music

### Accessibility:
- **Captions**: Auto-generated for all spoken content
- **High Contrast**: Ensure text readability on all backgrounds
- **Clear Fonts**: San-serif fonts minimum 24px size

### Analytics Tracking:
- **Completion Rate**: Target 70%+ story completion
- **Taps Forward**: Minimize to under 20%
- **Taps Back**: Encourage with compelling content
- **Exits**: Track and optimize based on drop-off points
