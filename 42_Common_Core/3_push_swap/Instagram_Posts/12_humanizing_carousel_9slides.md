### Design Philosophy:
- **Authenticity over perfection**: Real textures, human elements
- **Emotional journey mapping**: Visual progression through feelings
- **Inclusive design**: Accessible, welcoming to all skill levels
- **Personal branding**: Consistently human, never corporate
